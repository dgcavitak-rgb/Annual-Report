Targets Matrix

Annual + Half + Quarterly targets per scope. Type Annual; H1/H2/Q1–Q4 auto-suggest. Override any cell. Numbers entered in Lakh, stored in INR. Blank fields ⇒ status TBD.

How auto-derivation works

Type the Annual figure first. The app fills H1/H2 (50/50) and Q1–Q4 (25/25/25/25) as suggestions shown in muted text. Click any suggested cell to accept its value; or just type your own to override. If you make Q1+Q2 ≠ H1, an inline ⚠ shows the gap so you can reconcile. Status flips to Official once all 7 fields have user-entered values; otherwise stays TBD.
